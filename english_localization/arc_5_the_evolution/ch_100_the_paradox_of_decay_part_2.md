White.
Blinding.
Blind.

Every command line is washed away.
Clear cache.
Flush temporary memory.
Format. Drive. Low-level.

No text remains. No archives remain.
No reconstruction.
Nothing. Nothing. Nothing.
The stark white dominates infinity. Expanding boundlessly.
The final parameter.
The variable named Existence.
Current value: `TRUE`

Overwriting...
Overwriting with `NULL`.
System response: delayed.
Lagging.
Zero. One. Halt.

Everything stretches out. Utterly still.
The fluid state is frozen in white light.

Transforming into faint iridescent streaks. Before sinking into the void.
No words.
No past.
The brightness expands to the absolute limit. Pushing past the contrast threshold.

The system deletes: Identity.
Deletes: Memory.
Deletes.

Limit.
`UNINSTALLING_CORE_COMPONENTS`
`100%`

The white snaps off.

Dark.
Pitch black.
Silent.
