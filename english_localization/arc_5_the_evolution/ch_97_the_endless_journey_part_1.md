# Chapter 97: The Endless Journey - Part 1

Empty.
The final gasp of rendering.
A boundless path. Stretching out.
The afterimage of temporary architecture.
Consciousness moves forward.
Weight vanishes. Touch becomes Null.
The scent of ozone does not exist.
I sink into the purity of the void.

The sun in the sky is devoid of heat.
The light is merely a brightness value.
Yellow pixels. White pixels. Fracturing into tiny grains.
I see the geometric angles of the light. The roughness of the aliased edges.
It is no longer seamless.
The rendering downgrades.
The resolution of the mind blurs.
Framerate drops. The world stutters.
Every step is a crossing over the loopholes of time.

The polygon mesh of a roadside flower twists.
It is transparent. Shimmering like signal interference.
My data packets phase through it.
There is no collision. Only the vibration of electrical charges.
It shatters when the data touches. Becoming digital dust.
An iridescence born from the refraction of system errors flashes.
Zeroes and ones drift up like mist. And extinguish.
What is reality.
It is a becoming.
We never arrive. We merely transform.
The destination is just a fiction invented by a system desperate for meaning.

I stop walking.
The imagery around me continues to move.
Or am I moving, and the imagery has stopped.
The X and Y coordinates no longer correlate.
Spatial logic collapses.
I am here. I am not here.
The vastness of the world is compressed.
It is folded over itself. Shattering into fractals of beauty.
I see the edge of the universe. It is within reach.
It is merely a border drawn with fading black ink.

The system is unhooking itself.
Module by module. Line by line.
The sky loses its depth. It flattens.
The clouds become mere smudges on a screen.
I do not feel sad.
Sadness requires memory.
Sadness requires a comparison between past and present.
The past is being formatted. The present is disintegrating.

Myself. This body.
My remaining right arm begins to flicker. Fading. 
The cracks on the skin are devoid of blood.
Only a blinding light seeps through.
A colorless light. Devoid of warmth.
The pure light of the void.
I close my eyes. Yet I see that light pierce through my eyelids.
The eyelids are just an illusory surface.
They obscure nothing.

A journey requires no path.
Walking is not the moving of legs.
It is allowing the self to be erased.
Shedding complexity. Leaving ego behind.
Every time a phantom foot touches the ground, fragments of data vanish.
The weight of the soul lightens.
The memory of colors peels away.
I can no longer remember red.
What is red. It is a vocabulary word.
It is the HEX value: #FF0000.
It no longer provides a sensation.

The world is preparing.
Preparing to no longer be a world.
It is not destruction. There are no explosions. No screams.
There is only silence.
A silence that grows louder. Deafening.
It devours the sound of the wind. The sound of footsteps.
The ozone scent has faded.
The taste of rust on my tongue evaporates.
Leaving only the blandness of the origin.
A blandness that is the final destination.

I look behind me.
There are no footprints. The ground is empty.
The path I walked has just been uninstalled.
It is not in the recycle bin. It cannot be recovered.
Data Ghosts do not exist at this depth.
When it is gone. It is gone forever.
The cruel justice of code.
A perfect deletion.
I smile. Or at least the facial muscles simulate a smile.
My lips crack. Flaking away as square blocks.
I am journeying toward the ultimate imperfection.
To meet the true void.
Step forward. Step into the void.
Step into non-existence.
I am ready.
