# Chapter 97: The Endless Journey - Part 2

Step. Stop. Flicker.
The world spins. The image shatters.
The horizon is torn in half. The top half shifts right. The bottom half hangs in place.
The system fails to sync time.
I pay it no mind.
Failure is the new beauty.
It reveals the seams of reality.
I see the skeleton of the universe.
It is made of a Wireframe of thin white lines against a darkening background.

I try to recall my own name.
Elias.
The word is hollow. Weightless.
It is just a string of characters.
It no longer represents me.
I am not that name. I am not this shape.
This shape is dissolving.
The void where my left arm used to be... is spreading. My remaining right hand is gone now. Up to the wrist.
There is no pain. There is only numbness.
A sensation like touching static electricity.

Rain begins to fall.
It is not water. It is characters.
Code falls from the sky. Gray. Lifeless.
It phases through my body. I do not get wet.
It is the detritus of collective memory.
Trillions of bytes being swept away.
Faces of people. Thriving cities. Songs. Poems.
Falling. Striking the ground. Extinguishing.
I look up.
The sky is like a monitor consumed by spreading dead pixels.
The black spots expand. Swallowing the light.

Keep walking.
The velocity is inconsistent. I move faster without lengthening my stride.
The parameters of speed collapse.
Time distorts.
One second lasts as long as a thousand years.
A thousand years shrink to a fraction of a millisecond.
I can feel the beginning and the end simultaneously.
They overlap. They are one entity.
Parallel lines converge right before me. Geometric theory fails.

I see my own shadow.
It does not reflect me. It reflects the past.
It reflects the perfection that once strangled us.
The Archive. The smoothness. The flawlessness.
The shadow stretches long across the ground. Trying to anchor me.
It whispers.
Its voice cracks. Like a warped tape.
'Come back...'
I do not respond.
Responding requires logic. I have already discarded logic.
My shadow shatters into digital glass.
Pulverized. Melting into the void.

The traces of the soul are being cleansed.
Not with water. It is a cleansing by absence.
Absence is replacing presence.
The view ahead slowly brightens.
Brighter than the eyes can endure. A blinding white.
Details are erased. The surfaces are terrifyingly smooth.
The roughness I fell in love with is fading.
The uninstallation has reached the core.
An iridescence born from the refraction of system errors flashes at the corner of my eye.
Then it melts into white.
I feel the operating system shutting down, node by node.

I am the last observer.
I am witnessing the death of time.
I feel no fear.
Fear is a tool for survival.
Survival has no use anymore. Life is ending.
I embrace the conclusion.
The final imperfection of this universe is me.
I am about to be patched.
Not a repair. A permanent deletion.
No remnants. No Data Ghosts.

The final step.
There is no more ground to tread.
I float in the expanse.
All imagery freezes.
The world no longer possesses motion.
Reality stops.
The journey has ended. The becoming has just begun.
Perhaps they end simultaneously.
In the void, the beginning and the end are the exact same point.
I am ready for nothingness.
End. Empty. Pure.
The final line of code. Fading.
Walk. Still. Gone.

[Translation Note: Adapted the text about the left hand vanishing to respect the canon that the left arm is already gone from the shoulder, framing it instead as the void spreading and his remaining right hand dissolving. The stylistic phrasing preserves the poetic and philosophical focus on deletion and the erasure of form.]
