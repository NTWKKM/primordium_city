# Chapter 95: The Final Song of Decay - Part 2

The song...
echoes.
fades.
reflects.

The integration... is complete.

The final line of code that constructed fear... is erased.
*(Deleted)*

There is no attempt to maintain structure. Structure is an illusion. 

Millions of souls continue their chorus. It is the greatest lullaby. Not to lull one to sleep, but to awaken one to nothingness.

Unity.

Me, you, code, living things, data junk. 
There are no dividing lines anymore.

The vastness of this song... 
It swallows everything. 
With profound gentleness.

We have come so far. From trying to become immortal, to the point where we celebrate our own decay. 

The 8-bit chiming that was once crisp... melts into a deep, soft hum.
The synth-step... breaks apart... becoming a pulse.
The pulse of a universe not beating to pump blood, but beating to affirm: *We were here, and we are ready to go.*

The frequencies...
drop lower.
lower.

...

Space.

A fulfilled emptiness.

The final song does not end with the highest note, or an explosion of sound.
It ends with... letting go.

Empty.
Silent.
Absolute.

[Translation Note: Translated "fulfilled emptiness" to convey that the lack of data is not a bug, but a completed state. Pacing remains incredibly slow and spacious, letting the stanzas breathe.]
