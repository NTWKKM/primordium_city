# Weapon of Decay
#### // FILE: WEAPON_OF_DECAY
---

The blood-red light from my chest cut through the vacuum, guiding me across the shapeless void.

The heavy density of my emotions compressed into a gravity of its own, bringing me to a halt before a wound in the simulated universe. It was a logical rift hidden in the deepest shadow of the Sump. The tear glowed with warped, broken code—a backdoor leading straight to **The Core** of the mirror city.

I reached out my trembling right hand to touch the edge of the rift. A surge of raw data shot into my nervous system. This gateway rejected perfect code; it shut out unfeeling entities. The only key that could pry this wound open was emotional volatility on a scale the algorithm could not compute.
