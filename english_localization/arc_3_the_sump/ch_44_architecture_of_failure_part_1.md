# Architecture of Failure
#### // FILE: ARCHITECTURE_OF_FAILURE
---

Once we broke free from the plaza, the environment grew increasingly deranged. The surrounding architecture lost all structural consistency, geometric buildings shivering and **glitching**, morphing into erratic shapes.

Towering structures contracted and expanded with a complete lack of logic. Staircases climbing into the void folded back on themselves, hardening into solid walls. Narrow alleys stretched and squeezed like the trachea of some breathing beast. This was the architecture of failure, mocking every rule of existence.

The pathway shuddered. Instinctively, I slapped my right hand against a low-poly wall to steady myself.

*Zap!*

A high-voltage discharge of static electricity ripped through my palm, searing into my digital bones. I flinched, violently tearing my hand away. These walls had never been coded to register physical contact; any tangible interference triggered a short circuit, forcing my simulated nervous system to shriek in agony.

I held my breath, flattening myself to slip past the sharp geometric protrusions sprouting into the air. Every step through these skeletal ruins felt like treading on razor blades. The environment stood ready to slice my remaining bytes of data into trash pixels at any moment.
