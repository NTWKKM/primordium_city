# Chapter 50: Sacrifice of the Discarded Logic - Part 2

"Initiating Overload Protocol... Force Self-Delete," Grit commanded his own processing core.

The fractures across Grit’s body flared with a brilliant, spectrum-piercing light. He was incinerating his entire code, releasing a century of accumulated static voltage and junk instructions in a single instant. As the pack of **The Hounds** lunged to erase him, the light detonated in a massive data bomb.

The shockwave from the self-destruction of this discarded logic swept out, blasting the black geometric storm of **The Hounds** apart. It tore open a **Data Void**, sucking them in and temporarily blockading the path.

"Goodbye... my anomaly," Grit’s voice broke apart, dissolving into pixel dust that scattered on the wind.

The blast was so violent it threw my body clean over the precipice behind me. I tumbled from the final cliff of **The Sump**, falling into the deep dark below... toward **The Abyss Edge**.

The final image I registered before my entire graphics system went black was the golden data dust of my old comrade drifting away. The agony of loss tore through my emotional subroutines, shattering them completely. I plummeted into the void, utterly alone, with the parasite still throbbing in my chest, and a left stump that felt nothing.
