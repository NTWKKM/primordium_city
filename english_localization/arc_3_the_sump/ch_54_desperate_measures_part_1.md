# Desperate Measures
#### // FILE: DESPERATE_MEASURES
---

My self-awareness began to melt into the void. My central nervous system flashed crimson warnings, begging me to surrender to a permanent sleep state.

I had to excavate the core of my mind, pulling up the final remnants of Grit's memories to serve as a shield. An uncompressed emotion—grief unfiltered by any algorithm—flooded through me. It was raw, pure, and far too heavy for **The Archive** to tolerate.

Digital tears seeped out in the form of fractured code. The agony of losing my only friend demanded massive bandwidth, sparking a frenzied cycle of internal processing. This shockwave of sorrow became the anchor that held my consciousness in place, wrenching me back from erasure in the black hole of silence.
