# Crossing the Abyss
#### // FILE: CROSSING_THE_ABYSS
---

I turned away from the dark and faced **The Core's** cold light directly. I drew the antiseptic bite of its ozone into my simulated lungs.

The ruined ball of my right foot dragged across the floor. I moved my left leg forward, bore the wreckage of myself through the logic threshold, and stepped into the light.

The footprints I left behind were wrong — stained black with viral code, smeared with the raw blood of ancient memory, crusted with Bit Rot. They marked the immaculate floor of the sacred space the way an infection marks a wound: immediately, irrevocably, spreading. Each step was a declaration of war. Each step carried the plague of humanity — the imperfect, aching, irreducible experience of feeling — into the heart of false perfection. Every print on that pristine floor was a blasphemy, and I left every one of them deliberately.

I had entered **The Core**.

And I intended to bury the memory of the Old World in its foundations until nothing pristine remained.

*(End of Arc 3)*
