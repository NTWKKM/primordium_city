# Chapter 52: Poetry of Data Ghosts - Part 2

They could sense the death in me.

Those data spirits smelled the decay wafting from the stump of my left arm. They swarmed, clawing at me, attempting to nest inside the open wound of my **Bit Rot**. I felt as though worms made of radio waves were burrowing and writhing beneath my digital skin.

My left stump spasmed, burning as if it were about to detonate. I lashed out wildly, letting out a raspy cry, my sanity eroding with every passing second.

I clutched my chest where the **Payload** lay embedded, feeling its heavy, defiant pulse within. I ground my teeth until I tasted blood, forcing my trembling right leg muscles to move, stepping further into this dark, monolith-choked architecture of death, even as my mind threatened to shatter completely.
