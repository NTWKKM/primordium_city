# Architecture of Failure
#### // FILE: ARCHITECTURE_OF_FAILURE
---

The architectural madness outside was nothing compared to the horror erupting within.

The data key—the **Payload** embedded deep within my chest—began to throb violently. It was not the rhythmic pumping of a heart, but the erratic frequency of an overheating engine on the verge of exploding.

Heat spilled from the fissures beneath my skin. The Payload expanded and contracted violently, hammering against my simulated ribs until they cracked. I collapsed to my knees, clawing at my chest with my right hand, grinding my teeth so hard that a thick, off-color fluid resembling blood seeped from the corner of my lips.

It thrashed. It was trying to claw its way out.

It felt like a parasite chewing and tearing at me from the inside. It hungered for freedom, desperate to burst out and merge with the rot of the Sump, leaving me to endure the agony of my simulated viscera being ripped apart.

"Hold on, Elias!" Grit shouted, his rough hands shaking my shoulders. "Don't let it hijack your processing rights!"

I gasped for air, forcing what little willpower I had left to suppress its struggles. The pain spiked beyond all safety thresholds, my vision stained in the crimson glare of critical warnings before the hellish rhythm finally slowed. It left behind only scorch marks and deep, lingering trauma in my core system.
