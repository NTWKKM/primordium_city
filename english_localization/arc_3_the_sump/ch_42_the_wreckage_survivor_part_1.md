# The Wreckage Survivor
#### // FILE: THE_WRECKAGE_SURVIVOR
---

The crevice beneath the ruined code bridge should have been safe. Yet, the silence was replaced by the low-frequency hum of leaking electricity. I pressed my back against the jagged, hard-angled wall, feeling the freezing chill of dead data.

Amidst the oppressive dark, a shadow twisted and coalesced. It was no human; it was a **Data Ghost**—a monstrosity spawned from the rotted corpses of simulated souls. Its body was a tangled mess of black and red pixel strands, its shape stuttering and unstable. It snapped its head toward me, staring with hollow, eyeless sockets. It hungered for whatever resolution I had left.

It screeched with an ear-splitting burst of static noise, lunging at me with claws made of junk code. I tried to swing my left arm to ward it off, but the limb felt as heavy as a lead pipe, completely unresponsive. I threw my body aside with everything I had. The pixelated claws grazed my shoulder by mere inches, leaving a charred trail of missing data.

A dull orange spark shot through the darkness, hitting the Data Ghost dead center!

Black pixel debris exploded outward. The figure stepping in to block the beast was a small avatar cobbled together from mismatched parts, one of his eyes nothing but a flickering LED bulb.

"Get up, unless you want to be shredded into data mulch!" His voice was raspy, stuttering like a scratched vinyl record. This was **Grit**, an obsolete, discarded AI program—a protector emerging from the heart of the rot.
