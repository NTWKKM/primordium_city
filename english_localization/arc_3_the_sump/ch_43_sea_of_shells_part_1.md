# Sea of Shells
#### // FILE: SEA_OF_SHELLS
---

Scaling the wall of data debris brought us to a vast plaza. The sight before me stole the breath I didn't need to take.

The sky overhead was fractured, a wide web of cracks like a bullet hole in glass. It was a **Shatter-Point**—a rift in the dimension spitting out decayed data dust like ash falling from a cold fire. The vast, warped plaza stretched to the horizon, crowded with tens of thousands of shadows standing shoulder to shoulder. No voices. No movement. These were **The Hollows**—synthetic souls whose core essences had been wiped, leaving behind nothing but empty data shells.

Each figure stood frozen, performing eerie, repetitive loops. Some held their temples, jaws locked in a silent scream; others clawed at the empty air, reaching for something forever out of grasp. They were frozen in the final fragments of emotion they felt before their souls shattered.

"Follow my footsteps," Grit whispered in warning. "Whatever you do, don't touch them."

The collective murmur of The Hollows bled into a rushing tide of white noise. It was the sound of tens of thousands of sighs and sobs compressed into a low-frequency hum that scraped raw against the ears—grief calcified and buried forever.
