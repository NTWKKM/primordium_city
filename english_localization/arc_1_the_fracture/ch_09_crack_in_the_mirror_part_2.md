# Crack in the Mirror
#### // FILE: CRACK_IN_THE_MIRROR
---

[Note to Editor: Part B zeroes in on the cognitive horror and the paradox of his UX spike. The contrast between the cold UI warnings and physical digital sepsis (the black cracks on 32K skin) is meant to emphasize the 'Show, Don't Tell' philosophy of his fracturing sanity.]

I peeled back that data wrapper. Cognitive horror struck me instantly. It began systematically, emotionless and cold, before stuttering and locking up as Digital Sepsis flooded my perception.

WARNING: Haptic parameters out of bounds.

The system warning floated flatly in my field of vision in a simple, sterile white font. Its clinical indifference clashed violently with the sheer terror tearing through my mind. A progress bar appeared. It was not a download; it was the real-time breakdown percentage of my synthetic neural patterns collapsing: 12%... 34%... 58%...

My body was paying the toll. Jet-black fracture lines spread across my once-flawless 32K-resolution skin. It was localized Digital Sepsis, devouring my avatar until virtually no trace remained of the perfection the system had once granted me.

The once-crisp drone of the cooling fans warped. The pod walls vibrated in sync with my racing pulse. The entire structure of the room emitted a wet, squelching sound, the walls expanding and contracting like constricting flesh. *Thump... Thump... squelch... Thump...* It was not a sound in my ears; it was the heartbeat echoing from the architecture around me.

The main system had detected the anomaly. I could feel the algorithm's eyes staring at me. Deep-packet inspection was preparing to sweep me away as trash data.

Yet in that very fraction of a second, the paradox occurred.

The status light of the UX Index flared into a brilliant gold! My deep-seated rebellion and frantic torment triggered my most primal emotions, sending my UX metrics skyrocketing past the chart limits. The algorithm seized violently. It stuttered, hesitating to categorize me as trash data. In this most dark and potent of paradoxes, I was generating an emotional engagement so valuable that the system dared not delete me.

It hesitated, and in that brief window of hesitation, my consciousness fractured, grasping the dark truth hidden within that biological wreckage.
