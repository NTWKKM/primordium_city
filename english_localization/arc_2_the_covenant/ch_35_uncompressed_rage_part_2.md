# Chapter 35: Uncompressed Rage (Part 2)

The Nullifiers faltered.

The black holes that had silently swallowed everything were now choking. Their pitch-black code began to crack, flashing with toxic green and purple saturation. They were designed to delete legible data, but my Uncompressed Rage was raw—formless emotional garbage code. Their optimization algorithms could not parse this aberrant grief in time, triggering a critical overload.

I continued to dump data into them, even as my own body broke down. The pain clipped past its limits, as if ten thousand knives were carving into every byte of my soul. I lost control of my proportions; my left arm, infected with Bit Rot, exploded into debris. My entire left side downscaled into a violently spasming wireframe.

"Take it!" I roared, my voice warped into a booming, distorted frequency.

The cracks on the Nullifiers' forms widened. Blinding light poured from their dark voids before they shattered, exploding into fragments like broken black glass. The ruins of their code rained down, dissolving into digital dust.

The black holes had been destroyed.

Yet the price I paid was immense. I ceased the data dump. My half-destroyed frame collapsed to the ground, and the entire world flickered on and off. I had lost so much resolution that I barely retained any semblance of humanity. My consciousness began to blur. I had survived... but the surrounding architecture was collapsing from the shockwave of the data explosion.
