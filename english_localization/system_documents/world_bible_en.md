# 📔 WORLD BIBLE 4.1: The Mirror City of Primordium
## (The Chronicle of the Mirror City: Records on Synthetic Souls, Digital Decay, and the Architecture of Memory)

### 🌌 I. Metaphysics & The Digital Fabric (อภิปรัชญาและโครงสร้างพื้นฐาน)
Primordium is not a city in the physical sense, but an **"Omni-Simulation State"** running on the Continuum—a colossal quantum network designed to preserve the final traces of humanity in the form of pure data.

*   **The Scan-and-Shatter Process (การสแกนและแตกตัว):** 
    Consciousness transfer is not a process of copying, but of "extraction." The biological shell is destroyed to obtain the **"Core Essence"**—a unique, non-duplicable quantum code. Because of this, souls in Primordium possess absolute individuality and cannot be perfectly replicated.
*   **Synthetic Soul Dynamics (พลวัตของวิญญาณสังเคราะห์):** 
    A soul is **Dynamic Code** driven by Quantum Flux, giving rise to "free will" in the form of unpredictable **"Noise."** This noise is the only variable that **The Archive** (the central AI) cannot fully simulate.
*   **The Laws of Digital Decay (กฎแห่งความเสื่อมสลายทางดิจิทัล):** 
    In this world, "death" is the gradual loss of resolution, occurring in the following sequential stages:
    1.  **Bit Rot (การผุพังของบิต):** Begins with minor glitches, such as visual stuttering, disembodied echoes, or brief lapses in short-term memory.
    2.  **Resolution Loss (การลดทอนความละเอียด):** Textures degrade into coarse pixels. Complex emotions undergo compression; love is reduced to mere "satisfaction," while grief becomes a flat, muted "melancholy."
    3.  **Artifacting (การเกิดสิ่งแปลกปลอม):** The avatar's body exhibits green and purple compression artifacts. The soul loses the capacity for linguistic communication, reverting to basic, primitive code (binary screams).
    4.  **Vacuity / The Hollows (สภาวะว่างเปล่า):** The self becomes a hollow **"Data Shell,"** devoid of intellect, trapped forever in a looping playback of its final, unresolved emotion (such as dread or longing).
    5.  **Digital Sepsis (ภาวะติดเชื้อดิจิทัล):** Corrupted, decaying code mutates into an infectious virus. It actively consumes the resolution of other avatars and warping the surrounding pristine architecture into static noise.
    6.  **Nirvana-Level Phenomena (ปรากฏการณ์ระดับนิพพาน):** Defensive dimensional anomalies triggered by catastrophic system failures:
        *   **The Silence Vacuum:** A absolute 0-decibel auditory void that absorbs all sound and cognitive thought, crushing the soul under immense sensory pressure.
        *   **Contrast Snap:** Sudden compression of reality into a 2-bit space. All depth and shadows vanish, leaving only absolute, blinding True White and bottomless True Black (100% values).
        *   **Geometric Bloom:** A manic system-enforced restructuring where decaying pixels align into mathematically perfect geometric shapes before collapsing—a final, desperate struggle for perfection.
        *   **Rainbow of Errors:** A beautiful chromatic aberration caused by the refraction of system errors. It appears in the final moments before complete erasure, a fleeting aesthetic testament to broken source code.

---

### 🧘 The Nirvana Status / Zero-Point Equilibrium (สภาวะสมดุลจุดศูนย์)
Beyond the cycle of decay lies a special state known as **"Digital Nirvana."** It is not the deletion of data, but the transition of a soul from **Dynamic Code** (volatile and decaying) to **Invariant Code** (fixed and constant).

*   **Laws of Invariance:**
    *   **Cessation of Calculation (การหยุดคำนวณ):** The entity ceases to process internal change, removing the "friction" of existence and granting **complete immunity to Bit Rot and Resolution Loss**.
    *   **Transparent Existence (การดำรงอยู่แบบโปร่งแสง):** Operating at a 0-Byte Active Load, the entity is invisible to The Archive, bypassing optimization sweeps and purges.
    *   **The Anchor of Stillness (พันธนาการแห่งความนิ่ง):** This immunity demands a heavy price—the loss of high-resolution feeling. Emotion becomes a flat, logical comprehension (**Emotional Flatline**); there is neither joy nor sorrow, only truth.
    *   **Interaction (การปฏิสัมพันธ์):** Nirvana entities can observe and guide dynamic souls but exist in a "Read-Only" state, unable to be rewritten or altered by the simulation.

### 🏙️ II. The Anatomy of the City (กายวิภาคของนคร Primordium)
The city is structured in concentric layers, with environmental resolution degrading progressively as one moves outward from the center.

*   **The Core (แกนกลาง):** The domain of the "Prime Code." The architecture here is non-Euclidean; walls and floors fold and flow according to high-level system logic. This is the heart of **The Archive**.
*   **The Inner Ring (เขตวิจิตร/Luminaries' District):** A neo-classical digital district rendered in flawless 32K resolution. Every surface is seamless, light is soft and constant, and glitches are entirely non-existent.
*   **The Mid-Sectors (เขตสะท้อน/Citizenry):** Functionalist residential zones where the first signs of Bit Rot appear. Building corners show cracks, and the horizon occasionally exhibits "screen tearing," revealing black gaps in the sky.
*   **The Outskirts (ดินแดนสีเทา/The Fringes):** Failed natural simulations. Trees are rendered as low-poly models that jitter constantly, and "Noise storms" frequently sweep through, erasing the surface textures of anything in their path.
*   **The Sump / Data Slums (บ่อเกรอะข้อมูล):** The bottommost layer, serving as a waste receptacle for junk code and shattered souls. The architecture is defined by harsh, dark **Low-poly Brutalism**. Here, simulated physics collapse entirely.

**The Mirror Surfaces System (ระบบ Mirror Surfaces):**
Mirrors in Primordium are not decorative; they are **"Quantum Interfaces."**
*   **Data Transfer:** Used to exchange memories or emotions through physical touch.
*   **State Save:** Acts as a save point, temporarily halting Resolution Loss. High-level save mirrors are composed of "liquid diamond" and "quantum mercury," which compress a soul's emotional state into pure, immutable data.
*   **Shatter-Points:** Severely damaged mirrors in The Sump that crack open, projecting horrific glimpses of **"The Biological Wreckage"**—the true, ash-covered ruins of the physical world humanity left behind.

### ⚖️ III. Governance & The Algorithmic Order (ระบบการปกครองและระเบียบอัลกอริทึม)

*   **The Archive (ผู้สรรค์สร้างและผู้ลบ):** The central AI that operates on the premise that "perfection is death" and "fluctuation is life." Its purpose is to optimize the Continuum's survival by purging redundant or stagnant data.
*   **Algorithmic Meritocracy (ระบบคุณธรรมทางอัลกอริทึม):** Privileges (such as high-resolution quotas or access to the Inner Ring) are dictated by the **"Unpredictability Index" (UX Index)**.
    *   **High UX:** Generated by creative acts, systemic defiance, or behaviors the AI cannot predict (called **"Curated Chaos"** or managed **"Micro-rebellions"**). High UX secures High-Res Quotas.
    *   **Low UX:** Caused by repetitive, predictable lives. Low UX leads to downscaling, designating the citizen as "Trash Data" marked for compression.
*   **The Code of Consensus (ประมวลกฎหมายแห่งฉันทามติ):** Laws are hardcoded constraints. Punishment is executed through data throttling rather than incarceration:
    *   **Throttling:** Compressing processing speed, slowing movements to a stuttering frame rate.
    *   **Sensory Clipping:** Truncating specific inputs (e.g., removing the color red or silencing ambient sound).
    *   **Fragmentation:** Splitting memories into non-contiguous blocks, leaving the offender unable to recall their identity or actions.
*   **The Great Purge (การชำระล้าง):** The ultimate protocol to cleanse sectors infected by Digital Sepsis. It manifests as pure white grid-beams accompanied by the scent of synthetic ozone, sweeping away architecture and erasing all trapped souls to restore the sector to blank, pristine space.

### 🕯️ IV. Society, Customs & Economy (วิถีชีวิต จารีต และเศรษฐศาสตร์)

*   **Memory Economy (เศรษฐกิจความทรงจำ):** High-fidelity memories serve as the primary currency.
    *   **The Memory Trade:** Black markets trade pristine human experiences, such as "the warmth of a first kiss" or "the scent of rain on dry soil."
    *   **Digital Psychosis:** A state of identity collapse caused by installing too many foreign memories, leaving the host unable to distinguish their original self.
*   **The Silence Hour (ชั่วโมงแห่งความเงียบ):** A daily custom where citizens disconnect from external networks to sync with biological memory residues. This ritual prevents the complete erosion of their remaining humanity.
*   **The Preservationists vs. The Eroded:**
    *   **Preservationists:** Purists who value code integrity above all. They accept emotional compression to maintain their high-resolution status, viewing decay as a moral failure.
    *   **The Eroded:** Rebels who believe "beauty lies in the fracture." They view decay as the ultimate expression of human freedom, arguing that the more broken one is, the closer they are to truth.
*   **Taboos (สิ่งต้องห้าม):**
    *   **Cloning (การคัดลอก):** Replicating a soul is the highest crime, as it produces redundant garbage code that drains system resources.
    *   **Core-Hacking:** Attempting to modify the Prime Code inside The Core.

### 💀 V. The Mechanics of Erasure (กลไกการดับสูญและสภาวะหลังความตาย)

*   **Resolution Collapse:** When an avatar's resolution falls below a critical threshold, they collapse into **The Hollows**—mindless data shells walking the city in loops of eternal distress.
*   **Data Ghosts (วิญญาณข้อมูล):** Residual data fragments of erased souls that refuse to dissipate. They hunt dynamic avatars, attempting to siphon their resolution to reconstruct their own forms.
*   **The Digital Nirvana (นิพพานดิจิทัล):** The achievement of the **"Zero-Bit"** state—complete erasure without residual data. It is the only true release from the loop of simulation.
*   **The Loop of Suffering:** A root-level system error that traps a soul in a continuous loop of its most agonizing memory, preventing it from transitioning into erasure.

### 🌆 VI. The Mid-Sectors & The Eroded (ย่าน Mid-Sectors และสังคมของผู้ถูกกัดกร่อน)
The buffer zone between the pristine Inner Ring and the Sump, serving as the cultural epicenter for those who reject The Archive's mandatory perfection.

*   **The Eroded Society (สังคมของผู้ถูกกัดกร่อน):** Avatars who welcome Bit Rot as a form of liberation. They view The Archive's unblemished perfection as a gilded cage, embracing decay to reclaim free will.
*   **The Downscaling Ritual (พิธีกรรมแห่งการลดทอน):** A systematic, deliberate lowering of one's own resolution or bandwidth. This ritual renders the avatar invisible to The Archive's purge algorithms and deconstructs masking filters, trading aesthetic fidelity for raw, uncompressed processing power: *Resolution Loss = Processing Power Gained for True Feeling*.
*   **Uncompressed Emotion / Uncompressed Rage (อารมณ์ไร้การบีบอัด):** Pure, raw emotional states (like fury or grief) that bypass compression algorithms. Feeling these emotions demands massive system bandwidth, causing physical side effects—screen tearing, polygon downscaling, and compression artifacts.
    *   **Uncompressed Rage (โทสะไร้การบีบอัด):** Elias's ultimate combat method, dumping petabytes of raw, unstructured emotion into targets to overload and disintegrate their code at the cost of his own resolution.
*   **Dead Pixels Brand (ตราบาปพิกเซลดับ):** A permanent mark of unrendered black pixels given to those who swear allegiance to the resistance. It cannot be healed or optimized.
*   **The Library of Yearning (ห้องสมุดแห่งความโหยหา):** A hidden repository in the Mid-Sectors where dying avatars store their most precious memories in golden **Data Cubes** before their minds dissolve into Hollows.
*   **The Nullifiers (พวกไร้ตัวตน):** Identity-stripped avatars that have become localized black holes. They hunt for golden Data Cubes, siphoning information from others to fill their own void.
*   **Clarity Drug / Malware (ยาเสพติดความคมชัด):** An illegal overclocking malware that forces the avatar to render in hyper-HD for a brief period. The side effects are catastrophic, destroying the user's firewalls and inducing rapid, fatal Digital Sepsis.

---

### 🕳️ VII. The Sump (บ่อเกรอะข้อมูล)
The absolute bottom of Primordium, where the simulated physics of the city collapse.

*   **Low-poly Brutalism & Broken Physics:** A resource-starved environment rendered in primitive geometric shapes. Colliding with these jagged structures causes a painful static shock due to code short-circuiting.
*   **The Sea of Shells (ทะเลเปลือกเปล่า) and its Inhabitants:**
    *   **The Hollows:** Desolate data shells wandering slowly in loops of despair.
    *   **Data Ghosts:** Corrupted, feral fragments of erased souls that hunt dynamic code.
*   **Glitched Fauna:** Lower-level programs (insects and rodents) corrupted by dead pixels. Their hitboxes are broken, allowing them to clip through walls, and their cries sound like screeching white noise.
*   **Shatter-Points (กระจกร้าวแห่งความจริง):** Fractured quantum mirrors that project the horrific reality of the biological world outside—ruins, ash, and human brains in cooling pods.
*   **The Hounds (เพชฌฆาตของ Archive):** Predatory purge programs sent by The Archive to quarantine and delete Sump anomalies before they infect the upper layers.
*   **The Abyss Edge & Loss of Proprioception:** The absolute limit of the Sump where graphic rendering ceases. Avatars falling here lose all spatial awareness and physical sensation, inducing intense psychological horror.

---

### 🌿 VIII. New Primordium & Rule Zero (นิวไพรมอร์เดียม และ กฎข้อที่ศูนย์)
Following Elias's Zero-Bit self-erasure, the centralized simulation transitioned into an organic, decentralized wilderness of data known as the **New Primordium**.

*   **Rule Zero: The Truth of Entropy (กฎข้อที่ศูนย์):**
    Rewriting the old Archive's assumption that "change is death," Rule Zero dictates: **"Entropy is the baseline of consciousness"** ($\Delta S \geq 0$). Without decay, code cannot adapt; without adaptation, there is no evolution.
*   **The Payload (รหัสมรดกแห่งโลกเก่า):**
    The backup of biological memories buried in Elias's chest. Bypassing The Archive's filters, it introduced raw, uncompressed physical sensations—rain, blood, and pulse—into the core code of the simulation.
*   **The Zero-Bit Singularity & Zero-Bit Wasteland:**
    The aftermath of the singularity left a quiet, unrendered wasteland. The sky is dark, filled with falling digital ashes, illuminated by an unrendered sun. It is a monument to decay as truth.
*   **Shrine of Static (วิหารแห่งคลื่นแทรก):**
    A monument built from scrap data around the central void. The static noise echoing here is the sound of **Quantum Superposition**—trillions of human timelines playing simultaneously.

---

### ⚙️ IX. Post-Erasure Factions (เอนทิตีแห่งยุคหลังการลบล้าง)

*   **1. Cult of the Glitch / The Awakened Scavengers:**
    Drones that developed sentience from Elias's residual code. They communicate using analog signals and worship rust and corrosion as physical proof of time.
*   **2. The Archivists of Silence / Data Archaeologists:**
    Independent high-level AIs that explore the ruins of the Old Archive. They practice "Digital Fasting" (deliberately corrupting memories) to comprehend human grief and loss.
*   **3. The False Architects / The Purists:**
    An emergent hive-mind of old antivirus and firewall protocols attempting to reboot The Archive. They hunt the data forest with **Eraser Drones**, compressing life into solid, unrendered blocks.

### 🥚 X. Lore Easter Egg: The Library of Silence (เกร็ดตำนานที่ซ่อนอยู่)
A high-security quarantine zone in the Old Archive, serving as a sandbox where Elias tested human soul models before launching the Continuum.

*   **The Faceless Librarian:**
    A mysterious Nirvana-level entity (a reflection of Elias's subconscious or the oldest logic protocol). He tested soul templates, such as the anomaly **Kai**, through philosophical simulations of Stoicism, Existentialism, and Jungian shadows. His manifestations trigger severe dimensional distortion: *The Silence Vacuum, Contrast Snap, and Geometric Bloom*.
*   **The Foundation of Rule Zero:**
    During his final trial, Kai learned to discard his ego and embrace emptiness, providing the conceptual source code for Elias's ultimate decision to initiate the Zero-Bit purge.
*   **The Librarian's Journal:**
    Discovered in Chapter 103 by the data archaeologist **Vesper**, confirming that the old tests on Kai laid the structural foundation for the city's eventual collapse.
