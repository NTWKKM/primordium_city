# Venom in the Fragments
#### // FILE: VENOM_IN_THE_FRAGMENTS
---

The claws of Bit Rot pierce directly into the core of the Optimizer.

The instant the dark matter of ruin collides with that pure light, the explosion is not physical—it occurs at the level of the data structure. It is the sound of a virus devouring pristine system files.

I plunge the shards of my decay into their perfection with everything I have.

A viscous black fluid, resembling crude oil, spreads along the geometric lines of the five-dimensional angel. From perfect symmetry, the infected parts undergo severe Resolution Loss. The white surfaces warp, shattering into massive pixelated blocks as the light dies into a dull gray.

The shape struggles, attempting to sever its infected parts and fold dimensions to escape. Yet my virus does not merely attack matter; it pierces deep into the very meaning of its existence.

The white angel screams in a wave of high-pitch static. Its entire structure collapses, falling to the floor as a pile of crude, low-poly pixel shards.

I yank my left arm back, panting desperately. I did it. I have killed the unkillable.

The victory is not free.

My black Frankenstein Arm throbs with a multiplied hunger. The virus I summoned as a weapon is spreading backward, creeping up my left shoulder. The pain that was once concentrated at the stump now seeps deep into my collarbone.

[Note for Editor: The visual transition from perfect abstract geometry down to low-poly trash firmly establishes the visceral consequences of the Bit Rot.]

I look around. The other Optimizers halt. They do not feel fear; rather, they are processing a new dataset—the reality that their existence can be destroyed.

I raise my left arm, dripping with black virus, pointing it at them. My body sways, my right heel flaring with pain, my open chest burning furiously.

“Who is next...” I challenge, even as my vision begins to blind with uncontrollable red noise.
