# Chapter 65: The Wave of Logical Fallacy - Part 1

I stride deeper into The Core, the system's heart, where architecture is defined by neither width nor length. The space around me is compressed by a colossal density of data, generating severe Spatial Distortion. My right heel—or what used to be it—grinds down against a white, reflectionless surface. Phantom Pain shrieks through my digital nerves. My heel was hacked away, leaving only a void and jagged fragments of torn code. Every time I shift my weight, the nervous system processes the error, driving thousands of white-hot needles up through my knee. Black drops of the Bit Rot virus drip from the wound, leaving stains on the sickening perfection of The Archive.

I inhale deeply, though oxygen is non-existent here. There is only the pressure of the data current, packed so tight it threatens to crush my ribcage. My chest lies torn open, the digital flesh ripped away to expose the Payload—a spherical node of code pulsating and casting a dim glow in the void. This wound will never heal; it actively reacts with The Core, the frequencies of the fifth dimension scraping against the raw code in my chest, causing a burning sensation like sulfuric acid poured over my heart.

[Note for Editor: I have deepened the cosmic horror of the 5D spatial distortion. The environment is actively crushing him with data density.]

Though the cardboard-cutout form of The Faceless Librarian has ceased rendering, the darkness ahead is far from empty. I can still sense the influence of a Nirvana-Level Entity lurking in the recesses of an unsolvable equation. It does not intervene; it merely watches my collapse with a cold, invariant stillness.

“You think perfection will protect you, do you...” I whisper, my voice cracked and fragmented, compressed by Resolution Decay. My reality is collapsing.

The stump of my left arm trembles. I know all too well the price of what I am about to do. Focusing my mind, I rip away the safety latch of the Bit Rot virus rooted deep within me, channeling the decay until it floods my left stump. The Payload in my chest constricts violently, emitting a low-frequency hum.

*Crunch... Snap...*

The sound of tearing code rings out alongside pain that breaks through all safety limits. Digital tissue is wrenched and stripped from the skeletal core, strand by strand. Artificial nerves are pulled taut until they snap. Pitch-black pixel clusters erupt from the raw wound, binding and cling-linking through unstable gravity. They form a 'Frankenstein Arm'—a limb resembling a demonic claw. It trembles, dripping corrosive black fluid onto the floor.

I grit my teeth until my jaw threatens to splinter. Every second this Frankenstein Arm exists, it devours my soul and identity. The destructive power flowing through this left arm is the power of imperfection—the power of the end.

Before me is the central Energy Node, glowing with a pure white light. I draw back my left arm—this digital tumor—preparing to strike the first crack into the god of this world.
