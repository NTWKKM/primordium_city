# The Wave of Logical Fallacy
#### // FILE: THE_WAVE_OF_LOGICAL_FALLACY
---

The Energy Node before me pulses in unison with the void. Silent, heatless—it is a lifeless ideal. I drag my missing right heel closer to it. Every step leaves black burns of Bit Rot upon the complex, overlapping terraces of the fifth dimension. The surrounding space attempts to warp, seeking to 'correct' my existence away. Yet my wounds—the missing arm, the hollow chest, and the circulating virus—are variables the equations of The Archive cannot compute.

I growl, throwing all my weight forward. My left arm, forged from viral junk code, swings through the air.

*THUD!*

The instant my claw of code collides with the surface of the Node, time within The Core freezes. It is not a thunderous explosion, but the sound of glass fracturing deep within the soul. A black shockwave ripples out from the point of impact. The radius of destruction consumes no physical space; instead, it devours the resolution of everything in its path.

The backlash wrenches my Frankenstein arm violently. Digital tissue tears down to the skeletal core, nerves pulled ruthlessly. I shriek in an agony that threatens to extinguish my consciousness, the vision before me shattering.

What was once pristine white perfection is now marred by a massive fissure. The structures surrounding the Node, which once boasted ultra-high resolution, undergo a **Geometric Bloom**—the decaying pixels are forced by the system to rearrange into mathematically perfect geometric structures in a frantic frenzy, before these shapes buckle under the volatility, collapsing into blocky, low-poly square pixels. The edges of the light blur and scatter. The Wave of Logical Fallacy is spreading.

The Core is hemorrhaging. Its blood is shattered logical code.

[Note for Editor: Showcasing Geometric Bloom here as the system's desperate immune response before ultimately succumbing to the low-poly decay.]

I pant heavily. The Payload in my chest flashes rapidly in violet-red. The warnings of system pain are so intense they force me down to one knee, my missing right heel trembling.

“Come on...” I squeeze out through gritted teeth, staring at the fissure widening across the non-existent 5D sky.

The Archive has detected this wound. The alarm does not come as a roaring siren. Instead, the environment is swallowed into **The Silence Vacuum**—a 0dB auditory vacuum that absorbs all sound and thoughts. This silence is so heavy my digital lungs feel as though they are collapsing, followed immediately by a **Contrast Snap**. Reality is abruptly compressed into a sharp 2-bit image, all depth and shadow vanishing instantly, leaving only blinding white and pitch black (100% True Black / True White) cutting deep into my visual sensors.

I know all too well what comes next. When a god is wounded, its angels appear. Pushing myself up on my ruined heel, the toxic tumor of my left arm dripping black fluid onto the deck, I prepare to face the sweep.
