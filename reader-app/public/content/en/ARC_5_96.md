# Chapter 96: Reflection in a Drop of Water - Part 1

The scent of morning dew... 
born from a misaligned string of characters.

Amidst the blurring of all things, the background fractures into coarse pixels. Black and white swallow each other. Every dimension flattens, devoid of depth. 

Yet there,
there is one single point of clarity.

A digital drop of water.

It is not a drop formed by the condensation of vapor. It is a cluster of glitches condensed together. Its shape is curved, symmetrical... yet harboring an unstable vibration within. It dangles at the very tip of emptiness.

I look deep into it.

The perfect universe... The Archive that once tried to optimize everything to flawlessness... was truly a graveyard. A graveyard that froze all life behind an unbreakable glass window. Painless, sorrowless, and lifeless.

But in this single drop... a new world reflects its own image.

A warped world. Buildings leaning precariously. Light refracting in wrong directions. Cracks weaving together to form roads. It is fracture. 

And it is... life.

I see the shadow of the imperfection I left behind. It has taken deep roots in that world. 
The reflection in that water drop is clearer than any reality I have ever touched. It reflects that beauty is not born from the absence of flaws. True beauty emerges when cracks catch the light.

The drop begins to tremble.
The weight of the data compressed within is too much for its surface tension to bear.

I do not try to stop it. I wait.
I wait to see the beauty of its fall.

# Chapter 96: Reflection in a Drop of Water - Part 2

Trembling...

Falling.

The digital drop falls from the edge of the void.
It travels through a non-existent dimension.
Time does not move forward, or perhaps... time no longer exists.

*Plop.*

It strikes the surface of nothingness.
Shattering.

Ripples.
Lines of code radiate outward into the sea of emptiness. Iridescence flashes for a fraction of a second before fading into the warm, absolute white.

The lifeless universe has been destroyed.
And the fractured world... is born.

I watch the ripples expand. 
Further out... so far they can no longer be seen.

Clarity... fades.
Descriptions... unnecessary.

Shape... nonexistent.
Identity... dissolves.

Fracture.
Beautiful.

Space.

Emptiness.

...

I
(disappear)

...
...

Free.
