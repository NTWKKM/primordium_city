# Chapter 53: The Rift to the Core - Part 1

The deeper I stepped into **The Abyss Edge**, the more the architecture of death decayed.

The graphics rendering rate slowed to the point where physical logic broke down. The solid, angular pillars around me turned translucent, peeling away into absolute black void.

I was consumed by sensory deprivation. There was no concrete wall to cling to, no digital breeze, not even the faint echo of my own movement. This was a null-zone, absorbing all acoustic waves. Isolation wrapped around my broken body like a shroud.

The only illumination in this heavy darkness was the pulsing of the **Payload** embedded in my chest. It cast a crimson glow through my simulated ribs—a warped heartbeat reminding me that I was still alive.

# Chapter 53: The Rift to the Core - Part 2

My eyes strained to find direction, the darkness ahead so dense it threatened to crush my senses.

My missing right heel sent phantom pain jolting up my spine. My left arm, eaten away by the decaying virus, felt numb and as heavy as a lead pipe.

With the environment refusing to render, my proprioception began to fade as well. I could not tell where my fingertips were unless I forced them to move. Psychological confusion set in silently. The system was forcing me to surrender to the void, to become just another sleeping data shell.

The crimson light from my chest continued to ripple, bathing the fractured polygons of my skin. It was the sole compass left in a dead universe. I ground my teeth, dragging my crippled leg forward, breaking through waves of silence that stood ready to erase my existence at any moment.
