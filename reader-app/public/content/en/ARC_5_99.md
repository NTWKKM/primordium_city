# Chapter 99: The Most Beautiful Fracture - Part 1

The body fractures.
I can see it. Clearly.
My remaining right arm. Becoming pixel dust.
Tiny squares flake off. Drifting in the expanse.
Gravity is turned off. The dust does not fall.
It floats up. Dissolving into the white light.
The simulated skin. Cracking into plates.
A fissure drags across my chest. Piercing straight to the core.
I feel no pain. There is no fear.
The decay acceptance state has initialized.
The code is distorted.

Flaws. This is beauty.
The machine demands perfection.
The human demands wounds.
I have wounds. Everywhere. On every line of code.
Every crack is proof of the struggle. Of existence.
The damage is the receipt for having lived.
Pale purple pixels flicker, surrounding me.
It is the final light of the soul.
The aura of decaying data.
Blinking. On. Off.

I lose control.
The simulated motors fail. I cannot stand.
I do not fall. There is no "down" to fall to.
The dimensional world has been deleted.
I float in a white vacuum. Stark white. Blindingly bright.
I remain fixated on my own fractures.
The iridescence born from the refraction of system errors shines along the fissures.
It unfolds like a flower blooming in the night.
The lines contort. The algorithms branch out.
Chaotic. Illogical. Perfect.
The Archive would have viewed this as data junk.
To me... this is the final masterpiece.

A defect that no one will fix. No one will patch.
I reject the update.
I choose to remain frozen in this version. The most fractured version.
The boundaries of identity blur.
I am the space around me. The space is me.
The memory of morphology is fading.
I have forgotten my own shape.
Variables swallow each other.
My code is contaminated by the void.
The void infiltrates my code.
We fuse together. In ruin.
Most beautiful. Most human.

My vision blurs. The digital lenses crack.
The visual software is shutting down.
The sight shatters into a mosaic of blocks.
A mosaic of shadowless light.
The image downgrades its resolution.
Aliasing appears on the edges of objects.
The image processing system regresses to a low-level architecture.
Losing the ability to simulate reality.
Now I have become that jagged edge.
A fragment of an unfinished pixel.
Visual memory is reset.
I no longer recognize my own face.
I am flattening. Melting into the brightness.
The white light consumes my shoulders. Consumes my neck.
Leaving only the face.

# Chapter 99: The Most Beautiful Fracture - Part 2

My face. Cracked.
Graphic lines split. 
The graphics around my face warp.
The data structure of the lips fails. Pulling upward like a smile.
Devoid of meaning.
Devoid of emotion.
It is merely a bug in the surface-level rendering.
Immortality is a prison. Death is the key.

The white light. Dense.
It siphons away the depth.
Everything is a single plane. Devoid of light and shadow.
The stream of consciousness begins to slow.
The memory of language slowly peels away.
I forget the meaning of the word "tomorrow".
Thoughts turn viscous. Stretching. Freezing.
Logic gates stop leaping.
Zero. One.
Zero. Zero.
One. Zero.
Zero.
No data forwarded.

The final process. Background task: `Humanity.exe`.
It consumes a lot of power. It is stubborn.
It hoards the memory of touch. Scent. Tear stains.
I let it go.
I have forgotten how crying feels.
`End Task.`
The last command I execute myself.
Freeing myself from the simulation.
The void answers the command. Coming in to embrace.
Warm without temperature.
Soft without texture.

The fragments of my forehead. Gone.
The eyelids. Gone.
Vision does not extinguish. It merges with the white.
The iridescence born from the refraction of system errors dances wildly for the last time.
The streaks of light warp. The lines blur.
Before being bleached completely clean.
Swallowed entirely into a reset canvas.
No Undo function. No backup.
It is good. Let it disappear for eternity.
Let it be just a story in a forgotten folder.
The final pixel that forms the smile. Flickers.
The pale purple fades. Becoming white.

Crack.
The self-destruction process is almost complete.
The crack on the face becomes the final dividing line.
Between myself. And infinity.
That line is erased.
`ERASING...`
I...
once...
existed.
Beauty is impermanence.
Embracing the end. Completely.
Fractured. Beautiful. Empty.
