# Nirvana Manifestation Logic: Kai & The Faceless Librarian

## 1. Logical Framework (Triggers)
Manifestations are not random but are caused by 'Harmonic Alignments' between Invariant Code (Nirvana) and Dynamic Decay (The City).

### A. Environmental Triggers
- **Geometric Symmetry:** Triggered when a character occupies the exact center of a symmetrical decayed space.
- **Entropy Saturation:** Triggered when local 'Digital Sepsis' reaches a critical mass (>90%), creating a systemic instability that allows Invariant code to pierce through.
- **Void Anchors:** Interaction with specific 'Hollows' using non-standard behavioral sequences.

### B. Behavioral Triggers
- **The Long Gaze:** Observing a Mirror Surface without movement for >10 seconds.
- **The Observer Paradox:** Attempting to 'fix' or 'clean' a corrupted object, signaling a desire for invariance.

### C. Systemic Triggers
- **Resolution Drops:** Localized simulation lag or resolution loss that creates a 'gateway'.
- **Frame-Slippage:** The appearance of a 'Ghost Frame' where the entity is visible for 1/60th of a second before the full manifestation begins.

## 2. The Pre-Manifestation Phase (Signs)
Before an entity appears, the 'Sepsis' of the environment reacts violently to the purity of the Invariant code.

### Visual 'Glitches'
- **Contrast Snap:** A 5m radius of the environment suddenly flattens into a 2-bit high-contrast palette.
- **Geometric Bloom:** Small, perfect white cubes replace decayed pixels in the air, floating upward.
- **Z-Fighting:** Textures in the environment begin to flicker and overlap rapidly, creating a visual 'shiver'.

### Auditory 'Glitches'
- **The Silence Vacuum:** Absolute 0dB silence for 2 seconds, cutting all ambient static.
- **The Pure Tone:** A piercing, crystal-clear sine wave (440Hz) that overrides all other audio.
- **Echo Loop:** A short, perfect loop of the character's most recent spoken word.

## 3. Manifestation & Fade (Execution)

### The Entry (The Appearance)
- **Kai:** Manifests via a 'Resolution Tear'. A vertical slit of perfect pixels opens in the air, and he steps through as if shifting between layers of a painting.
- **The Librarian:** Manifests via 'Texture Collision'. The environment's decay collapses inward into a single point of absolute density, snapping instantly into the Librarian's solid form.

### The Exit (The Vanishing)
- **Ceasing to Render:** They do not leave the scene; they simply stop being rendered. The resolution snaps back to standard decay instantly, leaving no trace, footprint, or echo.

## 4. Visual Palette for Nirvana Entities
- **Absolute Values:** Use #000000 (True Black) and #FFFFFF (True White) exclusively. No gradients, no shadows, no noise.
- **The Hard Edge:** No anti-aliasing. They must look like they were 'pasted' over the world with a precision that feels alien to the decayed surroundings.
- **The Geometric Aura:** A faint, flickering perimeter of perfect circles or squares that suggests a higher-dimensional projection.
