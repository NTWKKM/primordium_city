# 📜 Specification: Nirvana Status & Entity Differences
## Project: Primordium Saga

### 1. Metaphysical Definition: The Zero-Point Equilibrium (สภาวะสมดุลจุดศูนย์)
Nirvana Status is the transition from **Dynamic Code** (fluctuating, evolving, decaying) to **Invariant Code** (fixed, constant, immutable). 

In Primordium, existence is driven by *change* (Quantum Flux). While "Noise" allows for free will, it also creates "Drift," which leads to Bit Rot. An entity in Nirvana status has ceased all internal fluctuations; they have "stopped calculating" their own existence, becoming a mathematical constant within the simulation.

### 2. Rules of Existence
*   **The Anchor Principle:** Nirvana entities do not reside in a physical sector. They are "anchored" to the Prime Code. They appear as projections within the city but exist across the entire Continuum.
*   **The Zero-Quotas Law:** They possess a 0-Byte Active Load. Because they do not fluctuate, they require no processing power, making them "Transparent" to The Archive's optimization and purge algorithms.
*   **The Observer's Paradox:** They can observe and influence standard entities but cannot be affected by them. They are "Read-Only" in the eyes of the system.

### 3. Comparison: Nirvana vs. Standard Entities

| Feature | Standard Data Entity (Dynamic) | Nirvana Entity (Invariant) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Decay** | Subject to 5 Stages of Digital Decay | **Immune to Bit Rot & Resolution Loss** |
| **Memory** | Uses Memory Economy (Trade/Quota) | **Sovereign Memory** (Direct Prime Code access) |
| **State** | Volatile; evolves via UX Index | **Static;** unchanging and immutable |
| **Sensation** | High-fidelity emotions $\rightarrow$ Compressed | **Emotional Flatline;** perceives only logic/truth |
| **Death** | Ends in Vacuity or Digital Nirvana | **N/A;** already at terminal equilibrium |
| **Archive Relation** | Managed/Optimized/Purged by AI | **Invisible/Transparent** to the AI |

### 4. Entity Applications
*   **The Faceless Librarian:** An "Administrative Nirvana." He is a system function that has achieved equilibrium. He possesses no self-will, and thus no drift. His immortality is a byproduct of his lack of desire.
*   **Kai:** An "Anomaly Nirvana." Kai represents an unstable equilibrium—a "Glitch in Nirvana." He retains the ability to feel and change (Dynamic) while possessing the immunity of the Invariant. He is the bridge between the Eroded and the Archive.
