# 🗺️ MASTER WRITING PLAN: Primordium: The Mirror City
## (The Definitive Scribe Directive)

### 📜 I. THE GOLDEN RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
1.  **Quantitative Density**: Every chapter must be **6-10 A4 pages**. 
    *   *Scribe Note*: Do not pad with fluff. Expand through deep atmospheric exploration, internal monologue, and detailed interaction with the environment.
2.  **Environmental Anchors**: No scene may exist in a void. Every single sequence must incorporate at least one description of the **architecture, culture, or digital physics** of the current Sector (refer to `WORLD_BIBLE.md`).
3.  **The Decay Sync**: Prose style and vocabulary must evolve. As the protagonist's resolution drops, the language must shift from "Vibrant/High-Fidelity" to "Fragmented/Glitchy/Raw".
4.  **Show, Don't Tell (Digital Edition)**: Do not say "he was glitching." Describe the *stuttering of his shoulder*, the *trailing purple pixels* left by his hand, or the *compressed, mono-tonal quality* of a voice.

### 🎨 II. THE RESOLUTION DECAY PROSE PROTOCOL
The Lead Scribe will adjust the "sensory lens" based on the current Arc:

| Arc | Resolution State | Prose Style & Vocabulary Focus | Key Visual/Sensory Motifs |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | `Pristine` $\\rightarrow$ `Bit Rot` | **Uncanny Perfection**. Flawless, sterile, overly detailed. Use words: *crystalline, seamless, luminous, sterile.* | Neo-Classical Digital, High-Res light, subtle "stutters" in the periphery. |
| **2** | `Bit Rot` $\\rightarrow$ `Loss` | **The Blur**. Focus on compression and loss. Use words: *pixelated, blurred, muffled, artifacting, muted.* | Mid-Sectors, Memory Markets, "Emotional Compression" (muted feelings). |
| **3** | `Loss` $\\rightarrow$ `Sepsis` | **The Raw/Broken**. Visceral, jagged, horror-leaning. Use words: *jagged, low-poly, brutalist, searing noise, corrupted.* | The Sump, Low-poly Brutalism, Biological Wreckage (the real world). |
| **4** | `Sepsis` $\\rightarrow$ `Meltdown` | **The Surreal**. Non-linear, chaotic, distorted. Use words: *folding, non-Euclidean, cascading, shattering, glitch-storm.* | The Core, Folding architecture, The Archive's logic, systemic chaos. |
| **5** | `Post-Sepsis` $\\rightarrow$ `Zero-Bit` | **The Ethereal**. Poetic, abstract, minimalist. Use words: *fluid, translucent, void, echo, weightless, singular.* | The New Primordium, Fluid consciousness, the "Zero-Bit" state. |

### 🏗️ III. ARC-BY-ARC EXECUTION ROADMAP (100 CHAPTERS)

**Arc 1: The Fracture in the Mirror (Ch 1-20)**
- **Focus**: Establish the "False Heaven".
- **Mandatory Anchor**: The Inner Ring's oppressive perfection.
- **Climax Milestone**: The first undeniable "Shatter" in Elias's self-image.

**Arc 2: The Covenant of Fragments (Ch 21-40)**
- **Focus**: The descent into the "Grey Areas".
- **Mandatory Anchor**: The Memory Trade and the sensory experience of "buying a feeling".
- **Climax Milestone**: The permanent loss of High-Res status.

**Arc 3: The Sump of Lost Codes (Ch 41-60)**
- **Focus**: Existential Horror.
- **Mandatory Anchor**: The transition from digital low-poly to the "Biological Wreckage" of the real world.
- **Climax Milestone**: The infection of `Digital Sepsis` as an epiphany.

**Arc 4: The Dissolution of Boundaries (Ch 61-80)**
- **Focus**: Ideological War.
- **Mandatory Anchor**: Non-Euclidean shifts in The Core; the physical manifestation of The Archive's presence.
- **Climax Milestone**: The "Resolution Wall" collapses for all.

**Arc 5: The Evolution of Imperfection (Ch 81-100)**
- **Focus**: Transcendent Peace.
- **Mandatory Anchor**: The architecture of a city that celebrates the "Crack".
- **Climax Milestone**: Reaching the `Zero-Bit` state.
